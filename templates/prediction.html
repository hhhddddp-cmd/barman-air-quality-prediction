Understanding the Parameters

SO2 (Sulfur Dioxide)

A gas produced by burning fossil fuels. High levels can harm respiratory systems.

NO2 (Nitrogen Dioxide)

Primarily from vehicle emissions. Can cause breathing problems and reduce immunity.

RSPM

Particles less than 10 microns that can penetrate deep into lungs.

SPM

Total suspended particles in air from various sources including dust and smoke.
